## How to Add Your Photos

### Required Image Files:

Add your photos to this `images` folder with the following names:

1. **adventure.jpg** - Our First Adventure
2. **smile.jpg** - That Beautiful Smile
3. **coffee.jpg** - Coffee Date
4. **sunset.jpg** - Sunset Together
5. **celebration.jpg** - Celebration Time
6. **hiking.jpg** - Adventure Time
7. **cozy.jpg** - Cozy Moments
8. **silly.jpg** - Silly Us
9. **dinner.jpg** - Special Dinner

### Image Specifications:

- **Format**: JPG, PNG, or WEBP
- **Recommended size**: 800x800 pixels (or any square ratio)
- **Max file size**: Keep under 500KB for best performance

### Where to Find Free Stock Photos:

If you don't have personal photos yet, try these sites:

1. **Unsplash** - https://unsplash.com/s/photos/couple
2. **Pexels** - https://www.pexels.com/search/couple/
3. **Pixabay** - https://pixabay.com/images/search/couple/

### Tips:

- Choose high-quality, romantic couple photos
- Make sure images are well-lit and clear
- Square images work best (1:1 aspect ratio)
- Rename your downloads to match the required filenames above

### Changing Image Names:

If you prefer different filenames, edit `gallery.html` and change the `background-image: url('images/YOUR-FILENAME.jpg')` part.
